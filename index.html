About CODE Institute

Pioneering Digital Excellence in Bagdogra, Siliguri

At CODE - Computer and Digital Excellence Institute, we are committed to providing top-tier computer education that meets the demands of today's rapidly evolving digital landscape. Located in Bagdogra, Siliguri, we offer a diverse range of courses from foundational computer applications to advanced programs in Web Development, Digital Marketing, and cutting-edge AI Prompt Engineering.

Our mission is to empower students with practical, job-ready skills through a comprehensive curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a team of highly experienced and dedicated instructors. We believe in fostering an environment of innovation and continuous learning, ensuring our graduates are well-equipped for successful careers in the tech industry. Join us to transform your passion into a profession and become a leader in the digital era.

What types of courses does CODE Institute offer?

We offer a wide range of courses including foundational computer applications (CCA, DCA, ADCA), specialized programs in Web Development (Full Stack), AI Prompt Engineering, and Digital Marketing & SEO. Visit our 'Programs' section for full details.

Are your courses suitable for beginners?

Yes, we have courses tailored for all levels, from absolute beginners (like our CCA program) to advanced learners and aspiring professionals. Each course description indicates its target audience.

Absolutely. We offer dedicated career guidance, resume building, interview preparation, and placement support to help our graduates secure rewarding positions in the industry. Our network helps connect students with potential employers.
